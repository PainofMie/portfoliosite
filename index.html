About Me

After almost 10 years working in business teams from different fields (ie:Service, Management, HR...),
I have come to realise the importance of data and how many companies and businesses fail to realise this.
From this I have studied a Bachelor's Degree in Data Analytics from the bootcamp Le Wagon in Paris and Google's Data Analyst course.
I'm now a Freelance Data Analyst, I help businesses make data-driven decisions to better guide themselves to success.

Since I was little I have always loved looking for and finding the details that others might miss.
This,plus my experience working in business teams, my knowledge of Python, SQL, and data visualization tools,like Power BI and Tableau has helped me to become a succesful analyst and solve challenges.

If you're interested and would like to work with me you can contact me by Email, on LinkedIn or Malt.

Services

As Freelance Data Analyst, I translate data into valuable and comprehensible insights.
My goal is to help make data-driven decisions to improve results, reach goals and save costs.

I am experienced in machine learning, automation, cloud-based tools and use data visualization tools to present the results.
I mostly use Python(Pandas,Numpy,SKlearn...), SQL, GCP (Google Cloud Platform), PowerBI and Tableau in my data projects.

  • Data Analysis

    I am specialized in analyzing data. Results, trends, outliers and recommendations are clearly presented in reports and/or tools.

  • Data Cleaning

    Using Python and/or SQL I gather and clean data to create reliable and useable tables for stakeholders and further analysis.

  • Data Analytics

    Get more value from your data by training machine learning models to better predict behaviors and patterns.

  • Dashboards

    Dashboards created with a data visualization tool will enable you quickly and easily analyze your own data in an interactive way.

  • Data Automation

    Setting up data pipelines between business tools and tables to make sure that the data is always fresh and intersting for analysis.

  • Cloud Data

    Setting up and using cloud-based tools to facilitate data maintenance. Centralization of data to get deeper insights on the business.
